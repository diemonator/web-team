What is Proxy?

Student Association for the English stream ICT students at Fontys University of Applied Sciences. Our purpose is to create opportunities and develop an environment for our students which is both academic and socializing profitable.

President

The President role is to ensure that all the departments work according to their planned schedules. He or she will also be the channel through which the department heads will get authorized for their budgets and expenses.

Internal and External

This departments role is described as administrative. It is responsible for the financial issues of Proxy and the paper work involved in those transactions. It will also assist in scheduling meetings that are compatible for all Proxy members.

Entertainment and Socializing

The entertainment and Socializing committee is responsible for organizing different social and educational events.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure department is in charge of maintaining and developing a proper structure for all of the associations online systems.

Teaching

The teaching department plays the role of creating workshops that are focused on helping students with different subjects of interests.

Assisting

The student assisting department provides help on Fontys Subjects of the Ict English stream course to all those students who need an extra hand.
